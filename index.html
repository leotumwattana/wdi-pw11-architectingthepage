Lesson: Wireframing and review

Lesson objectives

  • Learn the basics of website wireframing and why we do it
  • Wireframe a few pages
  • Learn a bit more about sectioning content and the <nav>, <article>, and <aside> tags
  • Get a little big deeper into ARIA roles
  • Take a bit of a break

Measuring successful completion of the lesson

Hopefully, you've come away from this lesson understanding:

  • How to create user personas and why
  • What the pros and cons are of personas
  • That the most important thing is not the personas, but understanding the needs and wants of your users
  • How to write user stories
  • That there are several different formats for user stories, and little agreement on how to do them, but near complete agreement that you need to do something to determine your websites's feature set and make sure it addresses the needs of your users
  • What a wireframe is, why it looks the way it does, and what it is used for
